Simple Recipe

Top Roman Noodles

3 minutes to prepare

This is a recipe from my childhood that has reoccured in my college life. Super easy quick meal that costs close to nothing to make

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Top Roman (noodles/seasoning)
  • Any other seasonings of your choice (season salt, obey, pepper, etc.) *
    • * optional

Instructions

  1. Boil water on stove top
  2. Place noodles in pot after water boils
  3. Let noodles boil with water for 3-4 minutes
  4. Strain about 90% of water from noodles
  5. Poor noodles in bowl and season to your taste
