Known for his bold aesthetics and applying the knowledge he gained from earning from Parsons School of Design and his San Fransisco upbringing, Preston now functions as an artist, creative director, content creator, designer and DJ. Since the launch and end of Been trill, a Lifestyle brand started by him and Virgil Abloh, Preston has emerged to be one of youth's culture most prised designer.
