A$AP Rocky

A$AP Rocky, is an American rapper, songwriter, record producer, record executive, director, actor and model from the Harlem neighborhood of Manhattan, New York. As the proclaimed face of A$AP Mob he has taken them to a new level from 2011-2017. Not only has Rocky and AWGE had the summer on smash musicly with "AWGEST" this year, they have also shooken up the streetwear industry with Midnight Rave pop ups and collaborations with Virgil Abloh's OFF-WHITE. Rocky's Third studio album is also set to drop before the end of 2017.
