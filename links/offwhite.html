Off-White

Defined as "Defining they grey area between black and white as the color Off-White", by its founder Virgil Abloh, Off-White has escalated to what some may call a fashion "powerhouse". Even in midst of criticism from mastermind designer Raf Simons, Off-white continued to excell. Collaborationg with contemporaries from A$AP Bari's VLONE to Nike, ending 2017 with the release of the "Top Ten".
