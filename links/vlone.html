VLONE

While the A$AP Mob is known primarily as a music collective, they’ve exemplified what the crew is capable of outside of the sonic realm with the introduction of clothing label, VLONE. Comprised of A$AP Rocky, A$AP Bari and CLOT’s Edison Chen. VLONE has worked with the likes of Virgil Abloh's Off White,No Vacancy Inn, and even had a collection with Nike, but that relationship was ended to to recent aligations against A$AP Bari.
